{# ── 1. SCOPE ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

01

Scope & Application

This Privacy Policy governs how TEKK Solutions Inc. ("TEKK Solutions", "we", "us") collects, uses, retains, and discloses personal information in connection with our consulting services, website (tekksolutions.ca), client portal, and any data-processing mandates we carry out on behalf of clients.

TEKK Solutions is subject to Quebec's Law 25 (An Act to modernize legislative provisions as regards the protection of personal information, S.Q. 2021, c. 25) and the federal Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) where applicable.

This policy applies to all individuals whose personal information we handle, including website visitors, contact form submitters, registered client-portal users, and individuals whose data is processed through our financial integration mandates.

{# ── 2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ────────────────────────────────────── #}

02

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to deliver our services and operate this website.

  • Contact form: name, email address, and the message content you submit. No additional personal data is requested or required.
  • Client portal registration: email address and a hashed password. We never store passwords in plain text.
  • Client portal usage: uploaded documents and files you share with our team, and the date/time of upload.
  • Server logs: IP address, browser type, and requested URL — retained for security and debugging purposes only, for a maximum of 30 days.
  • Financial integration mandates: when engaged to build data pipelines, we process client-owned operational and financial data (see Section 3 below). This data belongs to the client and is never retained by TEKK Solutions beyond the active mandate.

We do not collect sensitive personal information (SIN, health data, biometric data, financial credentials) through this website. We do not purchase, rent, or otherwise acquire personal data from third-party data brokers.

{# ── 3. FINANCIAL DATA PIPELINES ──────────────────────────────────── #}

03

Financial Data Pipelines & Client Data Processing

TEKK Solutions designs and operates automated data pipelines that transfer operational data (e.g., point-of-sale transaction records) into enterprise financial systems. When performing this work, we act as a data processor on behalf of the client, who remains the data controller for all personal information within those systems.

Point-of-Sale Platform → Encrypted Transport (TLS 1.3) → Transformation Engine → Enterprise Financial System

The following data-handling principles apply to all pipeline mandates:

  • Data minimisation: pipelines are scoped to process only the fields required for accurate financial reporting. No personal data beyond transaction identifiers and amounts is extracted unless explicitly mandated by the client.
  • End-to-end encryption: all data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Credentials and API keys are stored in environment variables — never in source code or version control.
  • No retention by TEKK Solutions: processed records are written directly to the client's target system. TEKK Solutions does not maintain a secondary copy of client transactional or financial data.
  • No third-party disclosure: client data processed through our pipelines is never sold, shared, licensed, or disclosed to any third party. It is used solely to fulfil the client's specific integration mandate.
  • Structured error logging: pipeline logs record operation status and error codes only — not the financial data itself. Logs are retained for 30 days and then purged automatically.
  • Contractual safeguards: all pipeline mandates are governed by a written data-processing agreement (DPA) that specifies the scope, retention limits, and security obligations of both parties, in compliance with Law 25, s. 18.3.
{# ── 4. COOKIES & SESSION DATA ────────────────────────────────────── #}

04

Cookies & Session Data

This website uses only strictly necessary cookies. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics platforms.

  • Authentication session cookie (Flask-Login) — created when you log in to the client portal. It is a session cookie (expires when the browser closes) or a persistent cookie if you select "remember me". It contains a cryptographically signed session token; no personal data is stored in the cookie itself. Legal basis: contract performance (Art. 12, LPRPSP).
  • Language preference cookie (Flask session) — stores your selected language (EN or FR) as a single string. It persists for 365 days so your preference is remembered on return visits. Legal basis: legitimate interest (improving user experience, Bill 96 compliance).
  • CSRF protection token (Flask-WTF) — a cryptographic token included in every form to prevent cross-site request forgery. It is a session cookie and does not contain personal data. Legal basis: security obligation (Law 25, s. 37).

All session cookies are flagged HttpOnly (inaccessible to JavaScript), SameSite=Lax (mitigates CSRF), and Secure in production (transmitted over HTTPS only).

You may clear all cookies through your browser settings at any time. Clearing the authentication cookie will log you out of the client portal.

{# ── 5. DATA RETENTION ────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

05

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as required to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by law.

  • Contact form submissions: retained for a maximum of 12 months from the date of submission, or for the duration of any resulting business relationship.
  • Client portal accounts: account data is retained for the duration of the active engagement plus 24 months. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8 — Your Rights).
  • Server access logs: retained for 30 days, then automatically purged.
  • Pipeline error logs: retained for 30 days per mandate cycle, then purged.
  • Client financial pipeline data: not retained by TEKK Solutions beyond the active pipeline execution cycle (see Section 3).

When personal information is no longer required and the retention period has elapsed, it is securely destroyed using industry-standard deletion methods. Electronic data is overwritten or cryptographically erased; physical documents are cross-cut shredded.

{# ── 6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES ──────────────────────────────────────── #}

06

Third-Party Services & Sub-Processors

TEKK Solutions uses a minimal set of third-party services. We conduct privacy impact assessments (PIAs) before engaging any new processor, as required by Law 25, s. 63.3.

  • Web hosting & infrastructure: our production environment is hosted on cloud infrastructure with data-centre locations in Canada (preferred) or within jurisdictions providing equivalent legal protection. A data-residency clause is included in all hosting contracts.
  • Google Fonts: the website loads typefaces (Inter, JetBrains Mono) from Google Fonts. This request exposes your IP address to Google. No cookies are set by Google Fonts. If you prefer, you may use a browser extension to block font requests.
  • No analytics, advertising, or social-media trackers: we do not integrate Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, or any comparable third-party tracking tool on this website.
  • No sale of personal data: TEKK Solutions does not sell, licence, or otherwise commercialise any personal information we hold, under any circumstances.
{# ── 7. SECURITY MEASURES ─────────────────────────────────────────── #}

07

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction, in accordance with Law 25, s. 37.

  • HTTPS / TLS 1.3 enforced on all connections; HTTP Strict Transport Security (HSTS) enabled.
  • Passwords hashed using a strong, salted algorithm (bcrypt/Werkzeug PBKDF2).
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers prevent code injection attacks.
  • CSRF tokens on every form submission.
  • Rate limiting on login, signup, and contact endpoints to mitigate brute-force attacks.
  • Rotating file-based application logs with access restricted to authorised personnel.
  • Environment variables used for all secrets — no credentials in source code.
  • Regular dependency audits and security patching.

In the event of a privacy breach that presents a risk of serious injury, we will notify both the Commission d'accès à l'information (CAI) and all affected individuals within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, in accordance with Law 25, s. 3.5 (amending s. 63.7 LPRPSP).

{# ── 8. YOUR RIGHTS UNDER LAW 25 ─────────────────────────────────── #}

08

Your Rights Under Quebec Law 25

As a Quebec resident, you hold the following rights with respect to your personal information. To exercise any right, contact our Privacy Officer at hello@tekksolutions.ca. We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days and respond fully within 30 days (extendable to 60 days with notice).

Right of Access

You may request a copy of all personal information we hold about you, the sources from which it was collected, the purposes for which it is used, and any third parties to whom it has been communicated. (LPRPSP, s. 27)

Right to Rectification

If information we hold about you is inaccurate, incomplete, ambiguous, or outdated, you may request that it be corrected. Corrections will be made promptly and communicated to any party who received the incorrect data. (LPRPSP, s. 28)

Right to Deletion

You may request that we delete your personal information when it is no longer necessary for the purposes collected, when you withdraw consent, or when collection was unlawful. Deletion requests are subject to any legal retention obligations. (LPRPSP, s. 28.1)

Right to Portability

You may request that computerised personal information collected from you be communicated in a structured, commonly used technological format, either to you or to a person or body you designate. (LPRPSP, s. 27, as amended by Law 25, s. 107)

Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing prior to withdrawal. Withdrawal may limit your ability to use certain features of our services. (LPRPSP, s. 14)

Right to File a Complaint

If you believe your rights have not been respected, you may file a complaint with the Commission d'accès à l'information (CAI):
cai.gouv.qc.ca  ·  1-888-528-7741
